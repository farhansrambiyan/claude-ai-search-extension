# Claude AI Search: Default Search & Context Menu

![Claude AI Search Banner](promo-banner.png)

> Transform your browser into Claude. Search from the address bar, or right-click text to Ask or Summarize instantly!

Tired of copying and pasting text into Claude? Want answers faster? **Claude AI Search** seamlessly connects your browser to Claude.ai, turning your address bar into a direct line to advanced artificial intelligence and bringing AI-powered analysis right to your fingertips.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. Address Bar Search (Omnibox Integration)
Instantly replace your default search engine with Claude AI. Simply type your question, prompt, or query directly into your address bar, press Enter, and Claude will automatically open and generate your answer. No extra clicks needed!

### 2. Smart Right-Click Context Menus
Highlight any text on any webpage, right-click, select **"Claude AI"**, and choose exactly how you want Claude to process it:
- **Ask** — Prompt Claude to explain, translate, or answer questions based on the selected text.
- **Summarize** — Instantly get a concise, easy-to-read summary of long paragraphs or articles.

### 3. Auto-Submit Magic
Unlike other extensions that just paste your text and wait, Claude AI Search actively **auto-submits** your query the second Claude.ai loads. You ask a question, and Claude immediately starts typing the answer.

### 4. Quick "ON/OFF" Toggle
Don't want to use Claude for a quick general search? No problem. Use our beautiful, minimalist popup (designed to strictly follow Anthropic's brand guidelines) to toggle the address-bar redirection **ON** or **OFF** instantly. When toggled OFF, your searches smoothly revert to your normal search engine without needing to uninstall the extension.

---

## 🚀 How To Use

1. **ADDRESS BAR** — Make sure the extension is toggled **"ON"** in the popup. Type your question in the Chrome address bar and hit Enter. Claude opens and answers immediately!
2. **RIGHT-CLICK** — Highlight any confusing text or long article, right-click it, hover over **"Claude AI"**, and choose whether you want Claude to **Ask** about it or **Summarize** it.
3. **TOGGLE** — Click the extension icon in your toolbar to easily turn the default search override on or off.

---

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. Claude AI Search is incredibly lightweight and secure.

- ✅ We **DO NOT** track your browsing history.
- ✅ We **DO NOT** collect, store, or sell any of your search queries.
- ✅ All queries are sent directly from your browser to your own signed-in Claude.ai account.

> **Note:** You must have an active, signed-in account on [Claude.ai](https://claude.ai) for this extension to work.
